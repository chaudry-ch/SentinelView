SENTINELVIEW — CONFIGURATION GUIDE
===================================

Before running, open soar/soar.py and fill in:

EMAIL_SENDER   = "your_gmail@gmail.com"
EMAIL_PASSWORD = "your_gmail_app_password"
               (Get this from myaccount.google.com → Security → App passwords)
EMAIL_RECEIVER = "your_gmail@gmail.com"

That is all you need to configure.
Everything else works out of the box.
